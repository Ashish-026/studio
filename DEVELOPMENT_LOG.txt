MANDI MONITOR - USER GUIDE & DEVELOPMENT LOG

1. QUICK START FOR NON-CODERS
---------------------------------------------------------
Step 1: Open the App and Login.
        - Admin: admin@example.com (Password: admin)
        - User: user@example.com (Password: user)
Step 2: Select your "Mill" and "KMS Year" (Season).
Step 3: Start entering data! Your data is saved automatically to the Cloud.

2. HOW TO ENTER DATA (STEP-BY-STEP)
---------------------------------------------------------
A. SETTING TARGETS (Admin Only)
   - Go to Mandi Register -> Target Allotment.
   - Enter the Mandi name and the total Quintals allotted by the Govt.

B. RECORDING PADDY ARRIVAL (Lifting)
   - Go to Mandi Register -> Paddy Lifted.
   - Click "Add Physical Entry".
   - Tip: Click the Calculator icon [🧮] to enter weights bag-by-bag. It will 
     automatically fill the "Paddy Received" and "Mandi Weight" fields.

C. MANAGING LABOUR
   - Add worker names in the Labour Register first.
   - When filling any form (Lifting or Processing), select the workers involved.
   - The system will split the total charge equally among them and update their balance.

D. VEHICLE LOGISTICS
   - If you use a "Hired" vehicle during Paddy Lifting, the system automatically 
     creates a record in the Vehicle Register. 
   - You only need to go to the Vehicle Register to record payments made to the owner.

E. STOCK & MILLING
   - Check the Stock Register to see how much Paddy is in your godown.
   - Use "Processing" to record milling. It converts Paddy into Rice, Bran, and Broken Rice.

3. GENERATING REPORTS (PDF)
---------------------------------------------------------
- Every Labourer and Vehicle Owner has a "PDF" button.
- Click it to download a professional statement showing all work, trips, and payments.

4. CORE ARCHITECTURE (CLOUD ENABLED)
---------------------------------------------------------
- Platform: Next.js 15 + Firebase Firestore.
- Database: All records are synced to the Google Cloud (Firebase).
- Reliability: Data stays safe even if you switch phones or clear your browser.
