1. HOW TO GET YOUR APP LINK
---------------------------------------------------------
Step 1: Look at the top-right corner of this screen.
Step 2: Click the button that says "Preview" or "Deploy".
Step 3: A new window will open with your app. Copy that web address (link).
Step 4: WhatsApp or Email that link to your mobile phone.

2. HOW TO INSTALL ON MOBILE (NO DOWNLOAD NEEDED)
---------------------------------------------------------
Step 1: Open the link on your phone (Chrome for Android, Safari for iPhone).
Step 2: 
   - iPhone: Tap the 'Share' icon (square with arrow) -> 'Add to Home Screen'.
   - Android: Tap the '3 dots' -> 'Install App' or 'Add to Home Screen'.
The app will now appear on your home screen with its own icon.
