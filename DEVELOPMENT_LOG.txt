
MANDI MONITOR - SEAMLESS SETUP & FIXES

1. RESOLVING 404 ERRORS
---------------------------------------------------------
- If you see "Page Not Found" or a 404 error on your mobile:
  1. Click 'PREVIEW' or 'DEPLOY' at the top-right of the editor again.
  2. Refresh your browser tab.
  3. Ensure you are using absolute paths (e.g., your-link.com/ instead of your-link.com/dashboard/settings).

2. SERVER-INDEPENDENT MODE
---------------------------------------------------------
- This app is designed to work WITHOUT a server.
- All data is saved to 'Local Storage' (long-term) and 'Session Storage' (temporary).
- NO CLOUD BILLING: 100% Free forever.

3. MOBILE INSTALLATION (Refined)
---------------------------------------------------------
- Android: Open Preview -> 3 Dots -> 'Add to Home Screen'.
- iPhone: Open Preview -> Share Icon -> 'Add to Home Screen'.
- The app icon is a professional Forest Green Mill icon.

4. AUTOMATIC UPDATES & LOGOUT
---------------------------------------------------------
- LOGOUT: Closing the browser tab or swiping away the mobile app logs you out automatically.
- UPDATES: Just open the app again to get the latest fixes. No need to reinstall.

5. DATA CLEARING
---------------------------------------------------------
- To wipe everything and start fresh, use the 'Clear All Records' button in Settings.
