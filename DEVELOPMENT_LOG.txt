
MANDI MONITOR - ZERO-COST SETUP GUIDE

1. WHERE IS THE BUTTON?
---------------------------------------------------------
Look at the VERY TOP-RIGHT corner of your web browser. 
- You should see a button that says "Preview" or "Deploy".
- If you don't see it, try zooming out (Press Ctrl and - on your keyboard).
- Once you click it, a new tab will open with your actual app.

2. HOW TO INSTALL ON MOBILE
---------------------------------------------------------
Step 1: Open the link from the Preview tab on your phone.
Step 2: 
   - iPhone: Tap the 'Share' icon (square with arrow) -> 'Add to Home Screen'.
   - Android: Tap the '3 dots' -> 'Install App' or 'Add to Home Screen'.
The app will now appear on your home screen with its own icon.

3. WHY THIS IS FREE (LOCAL MODE)
---------------------------------------------------------
- This app uses "Local Storage" (Browser memory).
- It saves data directly to your phone's memory.
- NO Cloud Billing, NO Credit Card, and NO Database setup required.
- Cost: 100% Free Forever.
- Note: Data is saved on the device you are using. It does not sync between different phones.
