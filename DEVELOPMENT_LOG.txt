2. REGISTER LOGIC & FORMULAS
---------------------------------------------------------
A. MANDI REGISTER
- Gross Weight = (Bag Count * Weight per Bag) / 100.
- Net Weight = (Gross Weight * 100 - Deductions in kg) / 100.
- UI Sync: "Paddy Received" maps to Gross; "Mandi Weight" maps to Net.

B. LABOUR REGISTER
- Worker Balance = Total Wages - Total Payments.
- Group Work: Automatically splits unloading charges across a crew.

C. VEHICLE REGISTER
- Logistics: Automatically captures trips from Mandi/Private forms.
- Formula: Balance = Total Rent - Total Payments.

D. STOCK REGISTER
- Processing: Subtracts Paddy and adds (Rice, Bran, Broken Rice).
- Yield % = (Rice Yield / Paddy Used) * 100.
