
MANDI MONITOR - USER GUIDE & DEVELOPMENT LOG

1. HOW TO USE ON MOBILE (INSTALLATION)
---------------------------------------------------------
This app is a Web App. You don't need the Play Store.
Step 1: Deploy/Publish your app to get a web link (URL).
Step 2: Open that link on your mobile phone browser.
Step 3: 
   - iPhone: Tap 'Share' -> 'Add to Home Screen'.
   - Android: Tap 'Settings (3 dots)' -> 'Install App'.
The app will now appear on your home screen with its own icon.

2. QUICK START FOR NON-CODERS
---------------------------------------------------------
Step 1: Open the App and Login.
        - Admin: admin@example.com (Password: admin)
        - User: user@example.com (Password: user)
Step 2: Select your "Mill" and "KMS Year" (Season).
Step 3: Start entering data! Your data is saved automatically to the Cloud (Firebase).

3. HOW TO ENTER DATA (STEP-BY-STEP)
---------------------------------------------------------
A. SETTING TARGETS (Admin Only)
   - Go to Mandi Register -> Target Allotment.
   - Enter the Mandi name and the total Quintals allotted by the Govt.

B. RECORDING PADDY ARRIVAL (Lifting)
   - Go to Mandi Register -> Paddy Lifted.
   - Click "Add Physical Entry".
   - Tip: Click the Calculator icon [🧮] to enter weights bag-by-bag. 
     - "Gross Weight" goes to "Paddy Received".
     - "Net Weight" goes to "Mandi Weight".

C. MANAGING LABOUR
   - Add worker names in the Labour Register first.
   - When filling any form (Lifting or Processing), select the workers involved.
   - The system will split the total charge equally among them.

D. VEHICLE LOGISTICS
   - If you use a "Hired" vehicle, the system automatically creates a record 
     in the Vehicle Register. You only need to go there to record payments.

4. CORE ARCHITECTURE (CLOUD ENABLED)
---------------------------------------------------------
- Platform: Next.js 15 + Firebase Firestore.
- Database: All records are synced to the Google Cloud (Firebase).
- Reliability: Data stays safe even if you switch phones or clear your browser.
